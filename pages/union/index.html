رسالة النقابة

نرحب بكم في الموقع الرسمي لنقابة السادة الأشراف الطالبين في السودان، البيت الجامع لأبناء السلالة الطاهرة من ذرية امير المؤمنين علي بن ابي طالب رضي الله عنه، والمرجع الذي يمد جسور التواصل والتعارف بين الأشراف في أرجاء الوطن.

نسعى من خلال هذا الفضاء الرقمي إلى:

  • توثيق تاريخ السادة الطالبين وتراثهم العريق.
  • تعزيز أواصر المودة والتكافل بين الأشراف.
  • خدمة المجتمع السوداني في ميادين الخير والعلم والعمل.
  • توثيق النسب الشريف وحمايته من التحريف او الضياع.

إن انتماءنا لهذا النسب الشريف تكليف قبل أن يكون تشريفاً، ومسؤولية قبل أن يكون مكانة، نستلهم منها قيم الحق والعدل والتواضع والإحسان.

The Union's Mission

We welcome you to the official website of the Hashemite Ṭālibī Union in Sudan, the unifying home of the pure lineage descending from the Commander of the Faithful, ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib (may Allāh be pleased with him), and the reference point that builds bridges of communication and acquaintance among the Ashrāf across the nation.

Through this digital space, we strive to:

  • Document the history of the Ṭālibī Hashemites and their noble heritage.
  • Strengthen bonds of love and mutual support among the Ashrāf.
  • Serve the Sudanese society in the fields of charity, knowledge, and work.
  • Document the noble lineage and protect it from distortion or loss.

Our belonging to this noble lineage is a responsibility before it is an honor, and a duty before it is a status. From it, we draw values of truth, justice, humility, and benevolence.

Who We Are

The Ṭālibī Hashemite Union in Sudan represents the unifying home for the pure lineage descending from the Commander of the Faithful, ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib (may Allāh be pleased with him).

Our body includes an elite group of noble Ashrāf across various states of Sudan, working to strengthen ties among members of the Hashemite family and to spread values of love, tolerance, and solidarity drawn from the legacy of Ahl al-Bayt.

We contribute to serving Sudanese society through religious, social, and cultural activities.

👥 Our Members:

The Union's council consists of:

  • The General Assembly.
  • The Executive Council.
  • The Head and Deputy Heads.
  • Specialized Committees: Scientific Committee, Genealogy Committee, Social Affairs Committee, Youth Committee, Women's Committee.

The Head of the Union: Dr. ʿUmar Mirġanī al-Qāsimī al-Ḥusaynī al-Hāshimī.

We are proud to unite all Ṭālibī Ashrāf under the umbrella of the Union, in a unity that preserves history and looks toward the future.
