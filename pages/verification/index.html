ملاحظة هامة: للحصول على شهادة نسب، يرجى التواصل مع مقر النقابة.

How Does the Verification System Work?

Certified Documents

The Hashemite Ṭālibī Union in Sudan issues official lineage certificates after thorough verification and scrutiny of family trees and genealogical chains. Each certificate is assigned a unique serial number that can be verified through this system.

Security and Reliability

Lineage certificates feature QR codes and encrypted serial numbers that ensure the document's authenticity and protection against forgery. Anyone can verify the certificate's validity through this secure electronic system.

Important note: To obtain a lineage certificate, please contact the Union headquarters.
